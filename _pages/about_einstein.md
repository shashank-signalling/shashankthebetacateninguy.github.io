Shashank Kumar is a Postdoctoral Scientist at the Institute for Molecular Medicine, University Medical Center, Mainz. His research focuses on signaling pathways and their roles in immune cell development and cancer. He have been recieved with an EMBL Travel Grant and previously completed a research internship at NIBB, Okazaki, Japan. His research to the field earned him the Senior Research Fellow award from ICMR and DAE-BARC. Shashank hold a Ph.D. Degree in Molecular Genetics from the University of Mysore.
