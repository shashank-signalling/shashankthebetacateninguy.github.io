This project, led by Nadine Hovelmeyer and carried out in collaboration with Matthias Bros (University Medical Center Mainz), focuses on understanding the role of Integrin Beta2 (CD18) in B cell development and function. Integrins are key regulators of cell adhesion and signaling, yet their precise contribution to B cell maturation and immune responses remains incompletely understood. This work aims to define how CD18-mediated signaling influences B cell development and function within the immune system
